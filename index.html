My adventure in Iceland

I recently fulfilled dreams of visiting Iceland. This fascinating country is truly a paradise for nature lovers. The country is known for its stunning landscapes, geysers, hot springs, waterfalls, and volcanoes. I was amazed by the beauty of the country. Some of the highlights from my trip



  • Hiked up to see stunning waterfalls which drops 60m over cliff

  • Went snowmobiling on the Myrdalsjokull glacier under the moonlight

  • Relaxed in warm, rich mineral-based lagoons of geothermal spa

  • Watched the mesmerizing Northern Lights dance across the night sky



    • It was an unforgettable experience and I can't wait to go back to Iceland again.
